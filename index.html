Welcome to

Empowering The Nation

Empowering the Nation has been at the forefront of transforming lives in Johannesburg. Founded by Precious Radebe, our mission is to equip domestic workers and gardeners with valuable skills, enabling them to secure better-paying jobs or even start their own businesses. Explore our courses and join the hundreds who have already empowered themselves and their communities.

Learn About Us

Who We Are

Empowering the Nation is a beacon of hope for domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg, South Africa. Established in 2018 by Precious Radebe, our organization is driven by a passion for empowering individuals who have often been overlooked in society. With a deep understanding of the struggles faced by many domestic workers and gardeners, Precious set out to create a platform that offers skills training and development, allowing these individuals to enhance their employability and quality of life. Our mission is to provide practical, marketable skills that can lead to better employment opportunities and entrepreneurial success, thereby uplifting entire communities.

What We Do

At Empowering the Nation, we offer a diverse range of training programs designed to upskill domestic workers and gardeners. Our courses are crafted to address the specific needs and interests of our community members, helping them become more versatile and marketable in the job market. We provide comprehensive six-month Learnerships that include First Aid, Sewing, Landscaping, and Life Skills, as well as six-week Short Skills Training Programs in Child Minding, Cooking, and Garden Maintenance. Our training programs not only enhance the participants' skills but also boost their confidence and self-worth, empowering them to achieve greater financial independence and career growth.

Join Us

Joining Empowering the Nation means becoming part of a supportive and transformative community. Whether you are an individual looking to upskill, an employer seeking to enhance the capabilities of your staff, or a volunteer wanting to make a difference, there is a place for you in our family. By participating in our programs, you are investing in your future and the future of those around you. Enroll in our courses, benefit from our expert training, and unlock new opportunities for personal and professional growth. Together, we can build a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
