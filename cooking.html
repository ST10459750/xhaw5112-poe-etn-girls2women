About This Course


Cooking

  • Purpose of Course:

    • To teach participants how to prepare and cook nutritious family meals, ensuring they understand nutritional requirements and cooking techniques.

  • Duration:

    • 6 Weeks

  • Fees:

    • R750

  • Content:

    • This course includes planning and preparing balanced meals, understanding nutritional requirements, and learning various cooking techniques. Hands-on cooking sessions will enhance participants' culinary skills.

  • Career and Business Opportunities:

    • Potential career paths include home cooks, personal chefs, or entrepreneurs starting a home catering business. Culinary skills are in high demand both domestically and commercially.
