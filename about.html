Our Story

Empowering the Nation was born out of a desire to uplift and empower individuals who have historically been denied access to formal education and skills training. Inspired by her parents' and relatives' struggles, Precious Radebe founded the organization in 2018 with a clear mission: to provide high-quality skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. What started as a small initiative has grown into a well-respected institution, known for its commitment to excellence and community development.

Our Journey of Hope and Transformation

Our journey is one of hope and transformation. Since our inception, we have trained hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners, equipping them with the skills they need to improve their livelihoods. Our programs have not only provided practical skills but also instilled a sense of pride and confidence in our participants. We believe that by empowering individuals, we can create ripple effects that positively impact families and communities, fostering a culture of growth and self-sufficiency.

A Community of Support

Precious Radebe's vision for Empowering the Nation is deeply rooted in her passion for social justice and community empowerment. Her unwavering dedication to improving the lives of others has been the driving force behind our organization's success. We are committed to continuing this legacy of compassion and excellence, ensuring that every individual who walks through our doors receives the support and training they need to thrive.

Our Mission

To provide practical, marketable, and comprehensive skills training to domestic workers and gardeners, leading and empowering them to seek and secure better employment opportunities and entrepreneurial success, thereby improving their quality of life and uplifting entire communities.

Empowering Through Education

Education is at the heart of what we do. Our comprehensive training programs are designed to equip participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in their chosen fields. From First Aid to Landscaping, our courses are tailored to meet the demands of the job market and the needs of our community. We believe that education is the key to unlocking potential and creating lasting change.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every domestic worker and gardener has the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances. We envision a future where skilled workers are valued and rewarded for their contributions, and where our training programs serve as a catalyst for social and economic change. Through our efforts, we aim to reduce unemployment, promote entrepreneurship, and elevate the standard of living for countless individuals and their communities. By continuing to expand our reach and enhance our offerings, we aspire to be a beacon of hope and a model of excellence in skills development and community empowerment.




Our Impact

The impact of Empowering the Nation can be seen in the lives of those we have trained and the communities we have touched. Our graduates have gone on to secure better-paying jobs, start their own businesses, and contribute positively to their communities. The skills and confidence they gain from our programs empower them to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for themselves and their families. Our success stories are a testament to the power of education and the resilience of the human spirit.

Join Us

Joining Empowering the Nation means becoming part of a supportive and transformative community. Whether you are an individual looking to upskill, an employer seeking to enhance the capabilities of your staff, or a volunteer wanting to make a difference, there is a place for you in our family.
